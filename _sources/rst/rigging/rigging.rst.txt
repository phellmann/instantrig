Rigging
======================================

.. image:: /img/rigging/main.png

InstantRig is a dynamic panel. Different rollouts and buttons will be enabled or disabled depending on which part of rig is selected in viewport. 

Arrows top of the panel allows user to dock panel left or right of max viewport.
